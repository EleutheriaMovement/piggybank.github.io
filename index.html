Introducing The
PiggyBank App

But what is it

PiggyBank is an innovative mobile app which will disrupt the space of interacting with money, that is to send, save, Invest, buy and pay bills.
This app has adopted the traditional way of saving money at home, which is convenient interms of having no charges and also to cut the hustle of having to go to ATMs and so on, only this time it is digital.

Download App Now

Save

Imagine having a R50 note and buy a voucher in your local store and redeem it in your PiggyBank app and just like that you have saved your money, you can choose to save it in different wallets. You can put it away for a desired period or even choose to get it in portions, for instance choose to get it daily for taxifare or weekly to pay your nanny

Send

Send money to your peers instantly without having to go to the mall and hold long queues, simply buy the voucher on your nearby spaza, redeem and send

Pay Bills

Pay your kasi bills such as your local burial schemes, buy tickets for local events, receive payments from your clients, buy and even borrow airtime and electricity. Having trust issues with a seller which you met online? no problem, PiggyBank can mediate by holding the funds until the product reaches you, if the seller fails to deliver, your money will return to your wallet.

Who We Are

PiggyBank is a product of Pleroma Systems Pty LTd, a software development company, which is focused in empowering people living in townships and rural areas by creating innovative systems that solve daily challenges that these communities face.

We are committed to service our community in building a sustainable financial stability on the previously disadvantaged individuals(PDI's). This is by far one of the most perculiar approach in curbing the circle of poverty. We want our community to learn to budget, and adopt the habit of saving and investing. We have identified that most solutions that involve the fintech are not tailor made to suit the needs of the dissadvantaged individuals

Frequently asked Questions

+ Is PiggyBank a registered bank??

The answer is no, PiggyBank is not a bank nor is it a financial institution, it is a section 27 organization according to the South African Constitution. This means it is a community based organization that just went digital.

+ How do I withdraw my money into cash?

PiggyBank App is embedded with the uber like algorithm that detects a member which is around who has cash in them and is willing to give to you with a fee which will be the profit for them.
